Simon Matthews music is a music production service based in Wellington, New Zealand. Simon has produced music for over 200 online videos, games and short films, as well as producing for some of the country's top recording artists.
A natural multi instrumentalist, Simon has composed commercial music for iconic brands such as BMW, Telecom and Kiwibank. Simon has composed and produced original music for soloists and produced vocals for artists - one of which has seen over 250,000 YouTube views.
One of Simon's main passions lies in film scoring, setting moods for scenes and adding that extra layer of depth to film.
